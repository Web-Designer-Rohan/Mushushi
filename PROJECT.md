# PROJECT: MASHASHI (The Solo Leveling System)

## 1. VISION & PHILOSOPHY
**Mashashi** is not just a self-improvement app; it is a forced evolution engine. Inspired by the "Solo Leveling" system and the Japanese concept of **Kaizen** (continuous improvement), the app is designed to push the user to their absolute limits across four pillars: **Discipline, Strength, Focus, and Wisdom.**

The philosophy is "Growth through Friction." The app acts as a digital "System Architect" that monitors the user's digital and physical life, rewarding elite behavior and penalizing stagnation through aggressive technical lockdowns and gamified progression.

## 2. CORE OBJECTIVES
* **Forced Growth:** Utilize system-level Android permissions to restrict access to distractions until physical or mental "quests" are completed.
* **Elite Stratification:** Move the user from "Rank E" (Commoner) to "Rank S+" (Elite) through a strictly verified XP (Experience Points) system.
* **Automated Accountability:** Use On-Device Computer Vision (Pose Detection) to ensure workouts are performed with integrity.
* **Minimalist Mastery:** Maintain a high-end, distraction-free aesthetic to keep the user in a perpetual "Flow State."

## 3. PRIMARY FEATURES & MECHANICS

### A. The Quest System (If-Then Routines)
* **Tech-Work Quests:** For every 30 minutes of productive tech/coding work, the system triggers a "Physical Tax." 
    * *Example:* Screen locks until the camera detects 20 pushups.
* **Elite Filter:** Only educational, tech, or growth-related content is allowed. If non-growth content is detected, the screen blacks out.

### B. Computer Vision Training (The Eye of Mashashi)
* **Automated Rep Counting:** Integration with ML Kit/MediaPipe to track skeletal landmarks.
* **Posture Integrity:** Reps do not count unless the full range of motion is detected. No "half-reps" allowed.
* **Visual Feedback:** Real-time posture correction overlays.

### C. Digital Shadow Realm (The Blackout Mode)
* **Strict Lockdown:** Automatic black screen during scheduled sleep hours or NSFW detection.
* **Emergency Access Only:** In lockdown, only the Phone (Dialer) and WhatsApp (for essential communication) are whitelisted.
* **NSFW Shield:** Real-time monitoring to ensure the user maintains the "Brahmacharya" path.

### D. The RPG Progression Engine
* **XP System:** XP is earned based on task difficulty and consistency (Progressive Overload).
* **Stat Tracking:**
    * **Strength:** Workouts, Reps, Calisthenics progress.
    * **Focus:** Pomodoro sessions, Deep work hours.
    * **Wisdom:** Journaling, Reading, AI-led reflections.
    * **Discipline:** Habits, Sleep cycles, Streak maintenance.
* **Rank Hierarchy:** E -> D -> C -> B -> A -> S -> S+.

### E. AI Intelligence & Journaling
* **Built-in Coach:** A conversational AI for reflection and motivation.
* **Progress Journal:** Support for progress photos, markdown notes, and annual navigation to track long-term transformation.

## 4. TARGET AUDIENCE
High-performers, "Vibe Coders," and individuals seeking an extreme structured life reset (90-day framework).

## 5. DESIGN LANGUAGE (The "Vibe")
* **Aesthetic:** Ultra-dark, premium glassmorphism.
* **Colors:** Pitch Black (#000000), Slate Grey, and High-Contrast White.
* **Typography:** Modern, clean, and bold (SF Pro / Inter).
* **Emotional Goal:** Focus, Authority, and Power.

## 6. PROJECT CONSTRAINTS
* **Platform:** Android (Primary focus due to system-level permission requirements).
* **Environment:** Termux (Kilo CLI) development with GitHub Actions for CI/CD compilation.
* **Performance:** All Computer Vision and monitoring must be on-device for privacy and speed.
